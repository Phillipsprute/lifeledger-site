Life Ledger Support

We are here to help. Find answers to common questions or contact us directly.

How do I add items to my inventory?

Open the app and tap any room to enter it. Tap the + button to add a new item. You can add a name, photo, purchase price, current value, and notes for each item. Your data saves automatically to your secure cloud account.

How do I use Life Ledger for insurance?

In the Insurance section of the app, you can assign items to specific insurance policies and track coverage amounts. This gives you a complete documented record that insurance adjusters can use when you file a claim.

How do I list items for sale?

From any item in your inventory, tap the sell option to mark it for sale and set an asking price. Your for-sale items appear in the Sales section so you can track everything you have listed and their total value.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you do not see it in your inbox.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your inventory is backed up to a secure cloud account using industry-standard encryption. We do not sell your data or share your inventory with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

To request account deletion, email us at support@lifeledger.app with the subject line Account Deletion Request. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
