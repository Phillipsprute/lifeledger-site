Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Life Ledger collects, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. What We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you create an account and use Life Ledger, you may provide:

  • Your name and email address (for your account)
  • Inventory data you enter (room names, item names, descriptions, values)
  • Photos you attach to inventory items
  • Any information you add when listing items for sale in the marketplace

Information collected automatically

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • App usage data (which features you use, crash reports)
  • IP address and general location (country/region)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Life Ledger app
  • Sync your inventory data to your secure cloud account
  • Send account-related notifications (password resets, security alerts)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your inventory data with advertisers.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Your inventory data is stored in a secure cloud environment using industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your data.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances: with trusted service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality obligations), when required by law or court order, or in connection with a business acquisition (with notice to you).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and export your data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@lifeledger.app.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Life Ledger is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the "last updated" date at the top and notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
