The Problem

Most people have no idea what they own — until it's gone.

Insurance companies ask for a detailed list of everything you lost. Most people can't even remember what was in their closet, let alone its value. That means they get paid less — sometimes thousands less.

🔥

House Fire

Without documentation, insurance adjusters rely on your memory. A panicked, incomplete list means a smaller check. Life Ledger keeps your proof safe in the cloud, untouched by flames.

🌊

Flood or Hurricane

When water destroys everything in minutes, the last thing you're thinking about is serial numbers and purchase prices. Have it all documented before disaster hits.

🔓

Theft or Burglary

Police reports require specifics. Insurance claims require proof. Without a pre-existing inventory, you're at the mercy of what you can remember under stress.

📦

Moving

Things get lost, damaged, or "misplaced" during every move. An up-to-date inventory protects you from movers who conveniently forget what they broke.
